# SOP Field Measurement MVP v0.1

1. Select farmer/plot.
2. Collect soil from the active root zone.
3. Remove stones, roots, and large debris.
4. Measure field-water pH and EC.
5. Mix soil 20 g with field water 50 mL.
6. Shake 2 minutes.
7. Rest 15 minutes.
8. Measure EC extract first.
9. Measure pH extract.
10. Record recent events.
11. Take photo if possible.
12. Save data.

Do not make lime/fertilizer rate decisions from one field-water pH value alone.
